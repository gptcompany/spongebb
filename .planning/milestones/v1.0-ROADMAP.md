# Milestone v1.0: MVP

**Status:** ✅ SHIPPED 2026-02-04
**Phases:** 1-10
**Total Plans:** 35

## Overview

v1.0 MVP delivers a complete global liquidity monitoring system based on Arthur Hayes' framework:
- Fed balance sheet data collection (WALCL, TGA, RRP)
- Global CB coverage (ECB, BoJ, PBoC, BoE, SNB, BoC)
- Overnight rates and FX collectors
- Market indicators (commodities, ETF flows)
- Capital flows and stress indicators
- Credit markets and BIS data
- Liquidity calculations (Net Liquidity, Global Liquidity, Stealth QE)
- Regime classification and correlation engine
- Calendar effects and FastAPI server
- Plotly dashboards and Discord alerts
