## Milestone Goal

Transform oil from "price tracker" to "macro liquidity indicator" with supply fundamentals, positioning data, and market structure analysis.

## Accomplishments

1. **EIA Weekly Petroleum Data** - Real-time US oil inventory, production, and refinery utilization tracking
2. **CFTC Positioning Analytics** - COT reports with commercial/non-commercial positioning and extreme detection
3. **Oil Term Structure** - Contango/backwardation signals from WTI futures curve analysis
4. **Real Rates Engine** - TIPS yields, breakeven inflation, and oil-rates correlation tracking
5. **Commodity News Intelligence** - Oil RSS feeds, OPEC calendar, NOAA hurricane tracker, supply disruption alerts
6. **Supply-Demand Model** - Balance calculator, inventory forecasting, oil regime classification (TIGHT/BALANCED/LOOSE)
7. **Combined Regime Integration** - Liquidity × Oil regime matrix for macro signals
